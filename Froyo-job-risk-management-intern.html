Risk Management Intern

Location: New York, NY | Team: Risk and Portfolio Controls

Job Description

  • Assist with daily risk monitoring across exposures, leverage, and concentration limits.
  • Support scenario analysis, stress testing, and drawdown attribution reviews.
  • Contribute to hedge effectiveness studies and volatility control frameworks.
  • Prepare recurring risk reports for portfolio managers and investment committees.
  • Collaborate with quant and operations teams on risk data quality controls.

Technical and Business Requirements

  • Understanding of portfolio risk metrics such as beta, VaR, and factor exposure.
  • Strong spreadsheet modeling and quantitative reasoning skills.
  • Basic programming ability (Python or R) is preferred.
  • Ability to summarize complex risk issues clearly for business audiences.
  • High integrity and disciplined process orientation.

Education and Major Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree or above (current students eligible).
  • Preferred majors: Finance, Risk Management, Statistics, Mathematics, or related fields.
