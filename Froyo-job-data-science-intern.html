Data Science Intern

  • Build and maintain data pipelines for research, monitoring, and reporting workflows.
  • Develop feature engineering frameworks for prediction and classification tasks.

  • Strong Python and SQL proficiency with practical data manipulation experience.
  • Working knowledge of supervised learning, model validation, and performance metrics.
  • Familiarity with visualization and reporting tools for communicating results.
  • Ability to translate business problems into structured analytical tasks.
  • Strong ownership mindset and comfort working in fast-moving teams.

Education and Major Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree or above (current students eligible).
  • Preferred majors: Data Science, Computer Science, Statistics, Applied Math, or related fields.
