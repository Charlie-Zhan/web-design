Investment Banking Intern

Location: New York, NY | Team: Venture Capital

Job Description

  • Support deal sourcing, pipeline tracking, and first-pass opportunity screening.
  • Build comparable company sets and assist with valuation and cap table analysis.
  • Prepare investment memos, committee decks, and market landscape briefings.
  • Coordinate due diligence streams across product, financial, and commercial workstreams.
  • Participate in founder calls and summarize key investment risks and catalysts.

Technical and Business Requirements

  • Strong Excel and PowerPoint skills, including financial modeling basics.
  • Solid understanding of accounting, valuation methods, and transaction dynamics.
  • Ability to synthesize large information sets into concise, decision-ready output.
  • Clear communication skills in both written and verbal business contexts.
  • High attention to detail and ability to handle multiple deadlines.

Education and Major Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree or above (current students eligible).
  • Preferred majors: Finance, Economics, Accounting, Business Analytics, or related fields.
