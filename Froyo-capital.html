Dual-Engine Capital Platform for the Future.

Froyo Capital is a global investment platform operating across Venture Capital and Hedge Fund strategies. We combine long-horizon innovation investing with systematic, data-driven public market execution.

Company Vision

Building a Long-Duration Investment Institution

Our vision is to become a premier multi-strategy investment institution that captures value creation across the entire technology cycle: from early-stage innovation in private markets to scalable alpha generation in public markets. We believe the future belongs to firms that combine domain expertise, systematic discipline, and global execution speed.

We are committed to rigorous research, responsible capital stewardship, and long-term partnerships with founders, institutional clients, and strategic ecosystem participants.

Company History

From Venture Roots to Integrated Capital Platform

Founded in 2016, Froyo Capital began as a frontier-focused Venture Capital firm investing in transformative technology categories. As our research and portfolio capabilities expanded, we developed deep internal expertise in quantitative modeling, market microstructure, and systematic risk management.

Over time, this capability expansion led to the launch of our Hedge Fund business, creating a diversified, dual-engine model where private market conviction and public market execution reinforce each other.

Business Model

Our two business lines are complementary in horizon, strategy design, and risk-return profile.

Venture Capital

Our Venture Capital platform invests in frontier technology companies with high potential for category leadership. We focus on AI, blockchain, autonomous driving, and industrial robotics, and actively support portfolio companies on strategy, hiring, commercialization, and follow-on financing.

The VC strategy is designed for long-term asymmetric outcomes through concentrated, research-led investment in technical teams solving durable global problems.

Approx. Capital Managed: $1.2B

Hedge Fund

Our Hedge Fund business runs multi-factor and multi-horizon quantitative strategies across global liquid markets. The strategy set includes AI-driven signal generation, high-frequency execution modules, index enhancement, CTA programs, and systematic hedging overlays.

The platform emphasizes repeatable alpha processes, strict risk budgeting, and portfolio-level drawdown controls, with continuous model monitoring and rapid adaptation to changing market regimes.

Approx. Capital Managed: $2.4B
