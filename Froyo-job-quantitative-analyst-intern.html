Quantitative Analyst Intern

Location: New York, NY | Team: Hedge Fund

Job Description

  • Research alpha signals using historical market, factor, and alternative datasets.
  • Implement and evaluate backtests with transaction costs, slippage, and risk constraints.
  • Contribute to strategy diagnostics including attribution, turnover, and decay analysis.
  • Support model monitoring dashboards and periodic strategy review packs.
  • Assist portfolio managers with regime analysis and scenario-based stress testing.

Technical and Business Requirements

  • Strong probability, statistics, and optimization foundations.
  • Hands-on coding ability in Python; familiarity with NumPy/Pandas is preferred.
  • Understanding of equity or futures market microstructure is a plus.
  • Ability to communicate quantitative findings to non-quant stakeholders.
  • Disciplined testing mindset and strong data quality awareness.

Education and Major Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree or above (current students eligible).
  • Preferred majors: Mathematics, Statistics, Financial Engineering, Computer Science, or related fields.
